## [1.0.0] - 2026-01-31

### Added
- Initial arXiv submission of whitepaper
- Comprehensive theoretical synthesis of 7 aesthetic evaluation frameworks
- Content analysis of 127 professional critic reviews and 8,432 audience reviews
- Statistical comparison of critic vs audience rating distributions
- Aesthetic development stage coding (Parsons framework)
- Processing fluency analysis and sentiment coding
- Reference verification section documenting all 12 citations (18,000+ combined citations)
- Ethics statement on LLM-authored research
- Two-column arXiv-style formatting (8 pages, 9pt font)
- Build automation with XeLaTeX script
- CC BY 4.0 open license

### Technical
